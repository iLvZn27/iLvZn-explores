Privacy Policy

iLvZn Explores does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information to any server. All data related to your visited states, notes, photos, and videos is stored:

  • Locally in your browser using IndexedDB
  • Optionally in your Google Drive if you choose to sync or back up your data

No data is uploaded to any external servers owned by the developer. All Google Drive actions are performed only with your consent and under your Google account.
